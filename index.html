Our Approach

We understand that life doesn't always go according to plan.

At West Island Rentals, we specialize in providing more than just a place to sleep. We provide stability during life's transitions. Whether your primary home is undergoing major renovations, you've experienced an insurance displacement, or you're navigating a personal transition like a separation, we are here to provide a seamless landing pad.

Every property is meticulously curated, entirely fully furnished, complete with all major appliances, and equipped with the fastest internet available (Starlink). All utilities (including hydro and electricity), as well as professional snow removal and landscaping, are completely included in your rate. Just bring your suitcase; we've taken care of the rest.

The West Island Standard

Uncompromising Amenities

High-Speed Starlink

Enterprise-grade internet in every property. Work from home seamlessly without buffering or dropouts.

Kitchens & Appliances

Fully furnished with premium cookware, utensils, coffee machines, and state-of-the-art modern appliances.

Dedicated Workspaces

Ergonomic desks and chairs provided. We understand that life transitions shouldn't disrupt your career.

Hotel-Quality Linens

Premium bedding, plush towels, and exceptionally comfortable mattresses for the restful sleep you need.

In-Suite Laundry

Modern washers and dryers in every unit, ensuring total convenience during your extended stay.

Smart Keyless Entry

Secure, personalized code access. No physical keys to lose, making move-in completely frictionless.

Full Backup Generators

Stay online and comfortable. Our properties feature full-home generators to keep power running seamlessly during outages.
