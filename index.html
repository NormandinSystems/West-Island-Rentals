Our ApproachNotre Approche

We understand that life doesn't always go according to plan. Nous comprenons que la vie ne se déroule pas toujours comme prévu.

At West Island Rentals, we specialize in providing more than just a place to sleep. We provide stability during life's transitions. Whether your primary home is undergoing major renovations, you've experienced an insurance displacement, or you're navigating a personal transition, we are here to provide a seamless landing pad across Montreal's West Island and surrounding areas. Chez West Island Rentals, nous offrons une stabilité lors des transitions de la vie. Que ce soit pour des rénovations, un sinistre ou une transition personnelle, nous offrons un point de chute idéal dans l'Ouest de l'Île de Montréal.

Every property is Green Certified, meticulously curated, entirely fully furnished, complete with all major appliances, and equipped with Starlink. All utilities (including electricity), as well as professional snow removal and landscaping, are completely included in your rate. Just bring your suitcase. Chaque propriété est certifiée écologique, entièrement meublée, avec électroménagers et Starlink.
